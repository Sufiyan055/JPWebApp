About
Tiara Consultancy

Tiara Consultancy Services , founded in 2018, is a leading recruitment firm dedicated to connecting businesses with top talent across various industries. We take pride in our specialized brand, GOHIREME, which focuses on domestic recruitment, ensuring the right talent meets the right opportunities. With a dynamic team of 82 skilled recruiters and a strong network of 300+ clients nationwide, we successfully close 450+ recruitments every month. Our reach extends to 500+ college campuses across Pan India, where we actively conduct training and placement programs, shaping the future workforce. Beyond domestic hiring, we specialize in international placements, particularly in Germany, helping ambitious professionals secure opportunities with global employers. Our mission is to bridge the gap between skilled professionals and international careers, ensuring a seamless recruitment experience for both employers and candidates.
